Parkrose Heights resource directory

Parkrose Heights is a neighborhood in northeast Portland. Its borders are I-84 to the north, NE Halsey to the south, NE 122nd to the east, and either NE 102nd or I-84 to the west. See here for a map of the boundaries.

Parkrose Heights Neighborhood Association

A new Parkrose Heights Neighborhood Association launched in 2025. Meeting dates and times can be found on the official City of Portland website. Currently they are scheduled for the second Tuesday of every month at 7:00pm at the Parkrose United Methodist Church in the Miller Hall building.

Earthquake and emergency preparation

  • "Basic Earthquake Emergency Communication Nodes" are locations in Portland where people can go to access emergency communications services if telephone service is down after an earthquake, or to report severe damage or injury. Knott City Park is Parkrose Heights's designated Basic Earthquake Emergency Communication Node. You might also want to download and print this map of other locations, in case the big one hits while you're in another neighborhood.

  • The neighborhood is covered by the Argay-Parkrose Neighborhood Emergency Team for earthquake preparedness. Visit their site for more information on how to prepare and how you can volunteer.
